# Math Escape

**Math Escape** is a 3D puzzle-adventure game where players solve math puzzles, collect coins, and navigate traps to escape a dungeon before time runs out. As my first game, it represents my journey into game development, blending creativity with technical skills to deliver an exciting challenge for players.

## Features
- **Math Puzzles**: Unlock doors by solving interactive problems.
- **Dynamic Obstacles**: Dodge moving traps and navigate the dungeon.
- **Time-Limited Gameplay**: Beat the clock to complete your escape.
- **Coin Collection**: Gather coins to satisfy the sorcerer’s demands.
- **Immersive Story**: Embark on a journey of redemption in a mystical dungeon.

## Story
You are a swordsman imprisoned in a dungeon by a vengeful sorcerer after a tragic accident. With your sword stolen, you must solve puzzles, collect coins, and evade traps to reclaim your freedom and prove your worth.
